ABOUT ME

Welcome to my site! My name is Tyler Ramsawak, and I'm a new student at the University of Florida *chomp chomp*. I plan on becoming an electrical engineer who designs new electronic circuits. I also really want to make my own ASICs by the time im 20. For a few years now, I've been designing my own electronic devices in KiCAD and fabricating them at home.

MUSIC

In january of 2025, I picked up the electric guitar and bass guitar. I've been practicing and playing all my favorite songs for a few months now, and I want to start song writing and form a band at UF. I loveeee indie, alt-rock, experimental, midwest-emo, all the emos, mall-rock, post punk, goth, dark metal, and so on. Anything with a guitar i love. Some of my favorite bands are Interpol, Radiohead, Weezer, and Arcade Fire. I think I would consider myself to be emo, but not goth. I sometimes dress up goth-y and I have been to a goth club, but Im more emo for sure. XxX
